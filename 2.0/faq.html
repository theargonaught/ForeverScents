F.A.Q.
What are the burn times for each size candle?
9 oz. burns for 40-50 hours
6 Cube block (Wax Melt) for up to 50 hours
Is it ok to ship soy candles in the summertime?
If you are shipping finished candles in hot weather, it may become a little too soft for the summer heat.
What kind of soy wax do you use?
We use Golden Brand Soy Wax and Ecosoya PB Pillar Soy Wax, which are all-natural soy waxes.
